🍽️ Your cinema diet

A region's canon is the set of films that region's own institutions celebrate — its top award rolls and critics' polls, not box-office or Hollywood's view of it. Every canon is a same-size plate: Hollywood's is exactly as big as Taiwan's. Coverage means: of the films that region's own canon celebrates, how many have you seen?
